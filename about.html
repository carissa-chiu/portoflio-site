About

Hi! I’m a UI/UX Designer and Web Producer based in Portland, OR.

Also a yoga instructor, musician, and climber; a big fan of nature, cats, and comic books (DC over Marvel). I love creating things - from handmade projects to websites and app screens. Through my diverse marketing background, I’ve learned the importance of establishing a brand voice and prioritizing the user experience. I carry this mindset into my design work - whether in creating a web page or mapping out the different user touchpoints of a campaign.

Previously created an ambassador program and influencer campaign for NaturallyCurly, and did web production at Entertainment Benefits Group. Currently managing digital website content at Dr. Martens.
